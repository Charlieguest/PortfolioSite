About Me

Hello my name is Charlie Guest. I'm a new graduate at Staffordshire University who is looking to start a career in the games industry. I am based in the North East of England.

I love designing and bringing to life creations for others to experience. Which has lead me to the games industry. I am passionate about communicating in a team to create games that are unique and interesting. My specialities in programming are AI and Procedural generation. I have experience in both Unreal Engine and Unity.

Although am looking for my first role in the games industry. I have previously completed a 2 Year apprenticeship in web development. This role required iterative development following the agile methodology, programming using JavaScript, HTML and CSS.

In my spare time i like to go hiking and am part of a 5 a-side football team in Middlesbrough.
